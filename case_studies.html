Case Study: UX Architect Banking App Client: Based in the San Francisco Metropolitan Area Launch Date: Based in the San Francisco Metropolitan Area

Bay Area Credit Union did not have a digital banking app and hired me as a UX Architect to do market research and work with the cybersecurity team to make sure the interface worked with their authentication servers. The first launch of the app includes Transfers, History, Deposit and Financial Planning. In January they will launch a newer version of the app that will include mortgage applications and loan application. I have already been contracted to complete the second phase of the project.

Market research included interviewing current clients as well as doing a competitive analysis. I had never worked on a banking application before and really enjoyed working with cybersecurity as this type of application relies on customers feeling this is a safe platform to use.
