About Ishaan Singh | UX Architect

In 2015, after graduating from Academy of Art University in San Francisco, I began my career is a UX designer. I soon discovered that I was more interested in the architecture behind the scenes. I pride myself on good communication skills and I have found that explaining why decisions are made to my clients is important, we need to work together and I love to collaborate with the team. I feel it’s important that every decision is informed and results in a good user experience.

I’m passionate about San Francisco architecture, veganism, and my Dog Annuk, aka my best friend in the world! If you are looking for a collaborative, imaginative UX Architect, please contact me. I would love to hear more about your project.
